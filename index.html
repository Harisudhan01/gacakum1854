About

The Government Arts College, previously known as the Government Arts College for Men, is an arts college located at Kumbakonam town in Tamil Nadu, India. It is one of the oldest and prestigious educational institutions in the Madras Presidency of British India. The Government Arts College was established in Kumbakonam as a provincial school on October 19, 1854.
